# Engineering Workbench Vision

## Vision Statement

Engineering Workbench is an open-source engineering platform designed to bring the tools, calculations, knowledge, and workflows engineers rely on into a single unified workspace.

Rather than replacing engineering judgement, Engineering Workbench empowers engineers by reducing context switching, improving traceability, and making engineering knowledge more accessible, interactive, and reusable.

Our vision is to become the open-source standard for practical engineering workflows.


---

# Mission

To provide engineers with a modern, modular platform that consolidates engineering calculations, project information, manufacturing tools, references, and technical workflows into one cohesive environment.

Engineering Workbench exists to eliminate unnecessary friction from engineering work, allowing engineers to spend more time solving problems and less time searching for information or moving between disconnected software.


---

# The Problem

Modern engineering workflows are fragmented.

A single task often requires switching between numerous applications, spreadsheets, PDFs, online calculators, standards, notebooks, CAD software, and web searches before arriving at a single answer.

This fragmentation results in:

- Lost time
- Repeated calculations
- Inconsistent documentation
- Knowledge silos
- Increased likelihood of human error
- Poor project traceability

Despite advances in engineering software, there remains no unified platform that supports the practical day-to-day workflow of multidisciplinary engineers.


---

# The Solution

Engineering Workbench provides one workspace where engineers can:

- Perform engineering calculations
- Record project information
- Access engineering references
- Compare design alternatives
- Estimate manufacturing costs
- Analyse quality data
- Document engineering decisions
- Generate professional reports
- Save engineering knowledge for future projects

Every calculation becomes part of the engineering process rather than an isolated result.


---

# Who We Build For

Engineering Workbench is designed around the interdisciplinary nature of Industrial Engineering.

Industrial engineers routinely bridge mechanical engineering, manufacturing, quality, operations, data analysis, project management, and continuous improvement.

By designing for this multidisciplinary workflow, Engineering Workbench naturally supports engineers across a wide range of disciplines, including:

- Industrial Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Manufacturing Engineering
- Product Design
- Quality Engineering
- Engineering students
- Engineering consultants
- Freelancers
- Makers


---

# Core Principles

## Unified

One workspace.

Many engineering disciplines.

Engineering should not require dozens of disconnected tools.

---

## Practical

Every feature must solve a real engineering problem.

Complexity should only exist where it creates value.

---

## Interactive

Engineering knowledge should be usable and reusable.

Calculations should be interactive, visual, and connected to projects — not static references.

---

## Transparent

Engineering decisions should always be traceable.

Every calculation should clearly communicate:

- Inputs
- Assumptions
- Formulae
- References
- Results

Nothing should be hidden behind a black box.

---

## Extensible

Engineering evolves.

Engineering Workbench should evolve with it.

The platform should allow new disciplines, modules, standards, and workflows to be added without redesigning the application.

---

## Open

Engineering knowledge grows through collaboration.

Engineering Workbench is committed to being open-source, community-driven, and openly extensible.

---

## Professional

The platform should feel at home in industry.

Whether used by a student, consultant, freelancer, or engineering team, the software should reflect professional engineering standards.


---

# What Engineering Workbench Is Not

Engineering Workbench is not:

- An ERP system
- A CAD replacement
- A spreadsheet replacement
- A document management system
- An AI decision maker

Instead, Engineering Workbench complements existing engineering software by providing a central workspace where engineering knowledge, calculations, and workflows come together.


---

# Long-Term Vision

We envision a future where Engineering Workbench becomes the engineering equivalent of an integrated development environment.

Just as software developers work within a unified development environment, engineers should have a unified engineering environment.

Over time, Engineering Workbench will grow into an extensible platform capable of supporting:

- Mechanical engineering
- Manufacturing engineering
- Industrial engineering
- Quality engineering
- Additive manufacturing
- Automation
- Project engineering
- Future engineering disciplines yet to emerge

without losing its simplicity or practical focus.


---

# Success

Engineering Workbench succeeds when:

- Engineers spend less time searching and more time engineering.
- Calculations become reusable rather than disposable.
- Engineering knowledge remains connected to projects.
- Students learn using practical engineering tools rather than disconnected resources.
- Engineers contribute modules that benefit the wider community.
- Organisations adapt the platform to their own standards and workflows.
- Opening Engineering Workbench becomes the first step in solving an engineering problem.
